How LyDia works

Our daily pick process.

LyDia is built around one idea: a pick should have a reason, a price, a timestamp, and a result. The process below is how we keep the daily slate consistent.

1. Build the slate

Load the daily MLB schedule, probable pitchers, team records, recent form, and available market odds.

2. Run the model

Use team strength, last-10 form, home field, starting pitcher adjustment, and no-vig market probabilities to create a first-pass lean.

3. Scan the market

Review public expert picks, odds movement, and sportsbook pricing to understand what the broader market is emphasizing.

4. Check context

Before publishing, review injuries, confirmed lineups where available, bullpen usage, travel, weather, park factors, and late scratches.

5. Pick only when there is a reason

A model lean alone is not enough. A published pick should have model support, price support, and a clear written reason.

6. Publish and grade

The free preview goes out first. Member picks include the full slate. Results are graded after games finish and kept public.

What makes a pick stronger?

SignalWhat it means
Model edgeLyDia's internal probability is meaningfully different from the no-vig market probability.
Price qualityThe available line still supports the pick. A good idea at a bad number is not a good play.
Context alignmentPitching, lineup, bullpen, weather, and park factors support the same side or total.
Market awarenessPublic expert analysis and line movement are reviewed, but they do not replace our own process.
Clear explanationThe reason can be explained in plain English before the game starts.

Transparency rules

No fake history

Results start from launch and are shown publicly. A 0-0 record is better than a made-up record.

No guarantees

Baseball has variance. Even strong processes lose. The goal is consistent decision quality, not guaranteed outcomes.

No blind copying

Public expert picks can be reviewed as research, but LyDia publishes original analysis based on its own model and written reasoning.

LyDia provides analysis and education only. Nothing here is betting, financial, or investment advice. No pick, model, stat, or market signal guarantees profit.
