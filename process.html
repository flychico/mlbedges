How LyDia works

Our daily pick process.

A pick should have a reason, a price, a timestamp, and a result. This is the operating system behind the daily slate.

1. Build the slate

Load the MLB schedule, probable pitchers, team records, recent form, and available market odds.

2. Run the model

Use team strength, last-10 form, home field, starting pitcher adjustment, and no-vig market probabilities.

3. Review public signals

Public competitor picks and market commentary are reviewed as research signals, not copied.

4. Check context

Look for injuries, confirmed lineups, bullpen usage, travel, weather, park factors, and late scratches.

5. Publish only with a reason

A model lean alone is not enough. A published pick needs price support and a clear explanation.

6. Grade results

Results are graded after games finish and kept visible.

LyDia is analysis and education only. No pick is guaranteed. Bet responsibly.
