How LyDia makes these picks

LyDia is a computer model, not a tout. Every game on this page is run through the same process:

  • Team strength from run-differential and recent form.
  • Starting pitching from earned run average and skill-based metrics.
  • Bullpen risk blending recent workload with how well the pen has actually pitched.
  • Market comparison against a no-vig probability from up to nine sportsbooks.

A game only becomes an official pick when the model probability and the Lab Rating both clear a fixed threshold and the price still offers value. Most games do not. On a typical slate LyDia passes on the majority, and those passes stay on this page with the reason attached.

Lab Rating grades setup quality on a 0 to 10 scale. It is not a win probability. A high Lab Rating with a low win probability is still a pass.

Frequently asked

Are these MLB picks free?

Yes. Every pick, every grade, and every pass reason on this page is free. Membership adds delivery before first pitch and the full member brief, not better picks.

Do you show your losses?

Yes. The record above counts every graded official pick, wins and losses. Nothing is removed after the fact. The results page holds the full dated history.

Can I use these for parlays?

You can, but understand what you are doing. Parlays multiply the sportsbook's edge along with the payout. LyDia grades single moneyline bets because that is what the model is built to price. Treat any parlay as your own decision, not the model's.

How often is this page updated?
