Kelly Criterion

Bet sizing from edge and odds. Most bettors use a fraction (¼–½) of full Kelly to reduce variance.

How to use these together: take the no-vig fair probability from the odds board as the market's opinion. If your own estimate (from stats, pitching matchups, etc.) beats it, run the EV tool. Only when EV is positive does bet sizing (Kelly) even matter.
