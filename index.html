Core product

A daily content operating system for MLB picks.

The goal is not to throw random picks at a board. The goal is to publish a consistent slate every day using the same repeatable process.

1. Model pass

Start with LyDia's own game model, team strength, probable pitchers, market probability, and value threshold.

2. Market scan

Review public expert picks and odds movement to see what the broader market is emphasizing.

3. Context check

Look for injuries, lineups, bullpen stress, weather, park environment, and matchup notes the model may miss.

4. Publish and grade

Send the free preview, deliver member picks, archive the preview, and grade results publicly the next day.
