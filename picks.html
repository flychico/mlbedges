Model Picks

A transparent lean for every game — no black box, the math is shown on every card.

How it works: each team's underlying strength is estimated from run differential (Pythagorean win%, which predicts future performance better than raw record). The two strengths are combined head-to-head (log5) and the home team gets the historical MLB home-field bump (~54%). When your Odds API key is saved, the model is compared to the market's no-vig probability — a pick is only flagged as Value when the model sees a meaningfully higher probability than the market price implies.
Crunching the numbers…
These are model outputs, not guarantees. The model knows nothing about today's starting pitchers, injuries, or weather — it's a baseline, and the market is usually sharp. Treat picks as a starting point for your own analysis, never as a reason to bet more than you can afford to lose.
