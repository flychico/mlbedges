Result tracking rules

Publish first

Picks should be posted or emailed before first pitch. No backfilling after games start.

Grade every pick

Wins, losses, pushes, and cancelled picks should all be listed. Nothing gets deleted because it lost.

Judge the process

Baseball variance is real. Results should be evaluated over a meaningful sample, not one hot or cold week.

LyDia provides analysis and education only. Results tracking is not a guarantee of future performance.
