# Responsible Gambling Rules

## Banned language

Do not use:

- lock
- guaranteed
- risk-free
- free money
- can't lose
- max bet
- unload
- mortgage play
- smash spot
- all-in

## Required posture

LyDia provides analysis, not guarantees.

## Required footer

```text
LyDia Picks provides analysis, not guarantees. Betting involves risk. Bet only where legal, responsibly, and within your limits.
```

## Do not

- Tell members how much money to bet.
- Promise profit.
- Present a pick as certain.
- Use desperation or pressure language.
- Encourage chasing losses.
