## Permanent rules

- No price edge = no official pick.
- Competitor agreement cannot create an edge.
- A pick must pass an internal price check, but public/paid copy should not show "Playable to" or "Pass at" fields.
- Reject contradictory same-game markets, especially Team A ML paired with Team B -1.5.
- Heavy favorites are usually better no-plays unless the market is still mispriced.
- Weather and lineup uncertainty should push totals/props into HOLD unless confirmed.
